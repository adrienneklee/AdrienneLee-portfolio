About Me

Been wearing my blue light glasses since 1988.

Oh, hello!
I’m Adrienne, a Front End Developer based out of Hamilton, Ontario with a background in sustainability and non-profit management. I love being able to bridge creativity and tech to build dynamic and meaningful web applications.

A recent grad of Juno College's Web Development Immersive Bootcamp, my passion for technology is bolstered by previous experience in corporate social responsibility and sustainability. I believe that tech can and should be transformative and accessible for all users, and has the ability to have a profound impact on people’s lives.

When I’m not coding you can find me hiking with my dog McGuffin, debating on the best movies of the 90s, or eating anything and everything with a pair of chopsticks.

Check out my tech stack and if you think I would be a fit for you team - let’s make something meaningful!

Safe Spaces Toronto

An application built with React, Safe Spaces seeks to provide safe restroom access for transgender, intersex, and gender nonconforming individuals. Safe Spaces utilizes data from the Refuge Washroom API, Geolocation, and Firebase.

Sun Run App

A group programming project built with React, Sun Run utilizes multiple APIs and Firebase persistence. Sun Run allows users to plan runs based on dates, sunrise/sunset times, distance, and pace. Made in collaboration with friends Sara Munir, Kelvin Lee and Dallan Jones.
